About Our Group

We are the STEM243 group, a team of dedicated students from Asia Pacific College. This website is part of our PETA (Performance Evaluation Task Assessment) for our Web Development class.

Our goal is to create a helpful resource for learning HTML5 and CSS3. We aim to provide clear explanations, examples, and practical information.
